Belching Beaver

04 December 2017 10:15PM


Hangover Rating:


There are 5 Belching Beaver locations around California. We went to North Park store located in 4223 30th Street San Diego CA 92104.

So, what alcohol do they have to offer?

They serve beers. Cold, worth-the-money, delicious beers. Acolohol content of each beer ranges from 5% to 8%.

My personal favorite? It's the limited, seasonal beer called "Hoppy Hoppy Night"

My cousins call it cider beer, I call it heaven. It is a 7% seasonal IPA with citrus and passionfruit twist.

Don't know what you'll like or not?

You can order their testers! You'll have 4 tester glasses with the beer that sounds amazing to you. Once you decide your favorite, order their full glass!

Oh! They serve beer floats too! Sounds weird, but trust me, it is delicious.

Overall, aside from the beautiful people you'll see in the bar, beer, service and ambience are great. We parked far though. Just ride an uber!

I rate this 4 beer mugs ot of 5 because the beer was great. It was so delicious, I think I had more than I could handle. By the end of the night, I can't remember anything. lol
